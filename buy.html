Selling a Dog

At Prasu's Pet Kin, we believe in finding loving homes for dogs in need. Selling a dog is a rewarding experience that changes both the dog's life.

When you sell your dog to us:

  • Save a life and give a second chance to a homeless dog.
  • Find a loyal companion who will bring joy and love to your home.
  • Support ethical breeding practices by choosing adoption over purchasing.
  • Help reduce the population of stray and abandoned dogs.

Steps to Adopt

  1. Explore our available dogs and read their profiles to find a good match.
  2. Fill out an adoption application form.
  3. Meet the dog in person (if possible) or virtually to ensure compatibility.
  4. Complete the adoption process, including a home visit if required.
  5. Bring your new dog home and provide love, care, and a safe environment.

Ready to change a dog's life? Start your adoption journey today with Prasu's Pet Kin!
